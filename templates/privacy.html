Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information to provide and improve the Service:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you register.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to operate and improve Spendly. Specifically:

  • To authenticate you and secure your account.
  • To display your expense history and generate summaries.
  • To diagnose technical issues and improve app performance.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal or financial data with third-party services. We may use minimal infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, and these providers are contractually required to keep your data confidential.

We do not integrate with payment processors, analytics platforms, or advertising networks that would receive your expense data.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you at any time. You may also request a copy of your data in a portable format.

  • To update your account information, visit your profile settings.
  • To delete your account and all associated data, contact us directly.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on our site. We will respond to all privacy-related requests within a reasonable timeframe.
